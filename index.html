Membership

We are accepting a maximum of 32 members to join in these first 4 boats. Each member will be able to use any of these 4 boats according to availability.

Each member is entitled for 2 pre-booking dates that are revolving once consumed. Any other booking will be a maximum of 48 hrs before use. Last minute same day booking will depend on what is available. A member can use the boat for 1 hour if she/he so wishes. There is no limitation on usage as long as the boats are used from sunrise to sunset. If a member would like to keep it overnight, she/he has to seek authorisation beforehand.

Fuel will be paid on consumption basis.

Visit and test drive

The boats are berthed at the MemberBoats Club basis within the Laguna Marina at Valetta Waterfront. We can organise a viewing and also a sea trial of the boats.
